## üöÄ Key Objectives

### üìà 1. Sales Performance Analysis
* **Customer Performance:** Detailed reporting to evaluate individual customer contribution and profitability.
* **Target vs. Actuals:** Comprehensive comparison of market performance against pre-defined sales targets (Volume & Revenue).
* **Strategic Growth:** Data-driven insights to determine effective customer discounts, facilitate negotiations, and identify expansion opportunities.

### üí∞ 2. Financial Reporting & Evaluation
* **P&L Statements:** Accurate Profit & Loss reports segmented by **Fiscal Year & Month** for temporal analysis.
* **Market-Level P&L:** Granular breakdown by Market to assess regional financial health.
* **Benchmarking:** Data for benchmarking against industry peers and historical periods to support budgeting and forecasting.
